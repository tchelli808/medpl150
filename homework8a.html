Story Description

My interactive story starts at the movie theaters. The setting takes a turn when the user watches a scary movie called “Laden at Midnight”. In the middle of watching the movie, they are teleported to the movie’s universe. Adhering to the movie’s plot, they become paranormal investigator who are ridding the city of its trapped ghosts. The various choices subject them to be a hero or a villain…
